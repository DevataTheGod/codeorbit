# CodeOrbit — Investor Deck

---

## Slide 1: The Problem

**The AI era broke learning verification.**

Bootcamps cannot verify whether students truly understand their work.

| Problem | Impact |
|---------|--------|
| Understanding unverifiable | 4-12 week gap between submission and interview |
| AI making assessment worse | ChatGPT, Copilot, Cursor enable fake project completion |
| Mentors don't scale | 20 sustainable, 50 difficult, 100+ impossible |

**Result:** Bootcamps graduate students who can't pass technical interviews. Employers lose trust. Revenue declines.

---

## Slide 2: Current Solutions Fail

| Solution | Why It Fails |
|----------|--------------|
| Manual code review | Doesn't scale beyond 20 students |
| Take-home projects | Can be completed by AI in minutes |
| Live coding interviews | Stressful, doesn't reflect real work |
| GitHub activity | Easily faked with AI-generated commits |

**None of these verify understanding.**

---

## Slide 3: CodeOrbit

**Learning Infrastructure Platform for Bootcamps**

CodeOrbit verifies student understanding at scale through:

1. **Understanding Score** — AI-powered comprehension measurement
2. **Reflection Challenges** — Forces students to explain their code
3. **Telemetry Engine** — Detects AI-assisted learning patterns
4. **Mentor Intelligence** — Scales mentorship to 100+ students
5. **Orbit AI Mentor** — Never writes code, always teaches

---

## Slide 4: How It Works

```
Student Creates Project
        ↓
Orbit Guides Learning
        ↓
Telemetry Captures Behavior
        ↓
Reflection Challenges Test Understanding
        ↓
Understanding Score Generated
        ↓
Mentor Reviews & Validates
```

**The score reflects real comprehension, not just completion.**
