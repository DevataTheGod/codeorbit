# Business Context

How CodeOrbit becomes a business.

---

## Customer Profile

**Primary Customer**: Emerging Bootcamp

| Attribute | Value |
|-----------|-------|
| Size | 50–200 students per cohort |
| Funding | Series A–B funded |
| Geography | India, US, EU |
| Problem Intensity | Very High |
| Innovation Appetite | High |
| Decision Speed | Fast (2 weeks interest → pilot) |

**Decision-Makers** (in order of influence):
1. Placement Head — Cares about job outcomes
2. Academic Head — Cares about curriculum quality
3. Lead Mentors — Cares about workload and effectiveness
4. Founder/CEO — Cares about margins and retention

---

## Pain Points

| Pain | Impact |
|------|--------|
| Unverifiable understanding | Can't tell if students really learned |
| Mentor overload | 50+ students, mentors burning out |
| AI disruption | Students using ChatGPT to complete projects |
| Interview failures | Students fail despite "completing" projects |
| Placement rate pressure | Job placement is core metric |

---

## Revenue Model

**B2B SaaS** — Bootcamps pay per student per cohort per year.

### Pricing Tiers

| Tier | Price | Duration | Students | Includes |
|------|-------|----------|----------|----------|
| Pilot | ₹2.5–5L ($3–6k) | 4–8 weeks | 30–50 | Full platform, weekly support |
| Growth | ₹99–149/student/year | Annual | 50–200 | Full platform, priority support |
| Enterprise | ₹250–500/student + 3% bonus | Annual | 200+ | Custom features, dedicated support |

**Freemium**: Free student tier generates bottom-up demand.

---

## GTM Strategy

### Primary Channel: PSIT Alumni Network

```
Week 1–2: DISCOVERY
├─ Find PSIT alumni at bootcamps (LinkedIn)
├─ Research bootcamp pain points
├─ Craft 20–30 outreach emails
└─ Goal: 5–8 mentor conversations

Week 2–3: VALIDATION
├─ Call mentors, ask about understanding verification
├─ Listen for pain (AI, mentor overload)
├─ Request warm intro to founder
└─ Goal: 2–3 founder intros

Week 3–4: CLOSING
├─ Pitch founder
├─ Show MVP demo
├─ Offer pilot: ₹2.5–5L
└─ Goal: 1 pilot signed

Month 2+: SCALE WITH PROOF
├─ Document pilot results
├─ Create case study
├─ Approach bootcamps 2–5 with proof
└─ Goal: 3–5 paid pilots, ₹15–25L revenue
```

## Pilot Structure

**Pilot IS the product research.**

| Week | Focus | Success Scenario |
|------|-------|------------------|
| 1 | Onboarding | 80%+ students active |
| 2 | Core Usage | Students asking Orbit questions |
| 3 | Mentor Adoption | Mentors checking dashboard daily |
| 4 | Measurement | Understanding Scores correlating with outcomes |

**Red Flags**:
- Mentor adoption < 50%
- Score not correlating with interview outcomes
- Orbit unhelpful or ignored
- Students not engaging with reflection challenges
