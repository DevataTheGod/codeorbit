# Product North Star

The product compass. What success looks like.

---

## Mission

Develop and verify independent software engineers at scale.

---

## North Star

**Engineering Judgment Verification**

Can the student independently explain and defend their project decisions?

Not: "Did they complete the project?"
But: "Do they understand what they built?"

---

## Primary Metric

**Understanding Score Accuracy**

Does the Understanding Score reliably predict:
- Interview performance
- Mentored concept mastery
- Independent problem-solving ability

Target: 75%+ correlation with mentor assessments by Month 6.

---

## Secondary Metrics

| Metric | Target | Why It Matters |
|--------|--------|----------------|
| Mentor Efficiency | 50+ students per mentor | Scales the business |
| Reflection Completion Rate | 80%+ | Verifies engagement |
| Placement Outcomes | 10%+ improvement | Customer value |
| Student Retention | 90%+ | Product stickiness |
| Orbit Engagement Quality | 4+ questions per session | Learning depth |
| Time to First Mentor Intervention | < 2 days | Risk detection |

## Product Hierarchy

```
1. Understanding Score
   ↓
2. Reflection Challenges
   ↓
3. Mentor Dashboard
   ↓
4. Orbit Mentor
   ↓
5. Roadmap Generator
   ↓
6. Telemetry Engine
   ↓
7. Browser IDE
```

**Rationale**: Items 1-4 are the moat. Items 5-6 support the moat. Item 7 is infrastructure.

---

## Success Definition

### For Students
"I can explain every decision I made in my project."

### For Mentors
"I know exactly which students need help and why."

### For Bootcamps
"I can predict placement success before interviews."

### For CodeOrbit
"The Understanding Score is the industry standard for engineering judgment."

---

## Anti-Metrics

What we DO NOT optimize:

| Anti-Metric | Why |
|-------------|-----|
| Total code lines written | Volume ≠ understanding |
| Number of features shipped | Speed ≠ quality |
| Time spent in platform | Time ≠ learning |
| IDE complexity | Tool ≠ skill |
| Number of commits | Activity ≠ progress |

## Decision Filter

Every feature must answer:

1. Does this improve **Understanding Score Accuracy**?
2. Does this improve **Mentor Efficiency**?
3. Does this improve **Student Learning Outcomes**?
4. Does this improve **Placement Success**?

If NO to all: **Don't build it.**
