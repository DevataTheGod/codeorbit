# Business Strategy

How CodeOrbit becomes a sustainable business.

---

## Target Customer

**Emerging Bootcamps** (50-200 students/cohort)

| Attribute | Value |
|-----------|-------|
| Size | 50-200 students |
| Funding | Series A-B |
| Geography | India, US, EU |
| Problem Intensity | Very High |
| Decision Speed | Fast (2 weeks) |

**NOT targeting**: Universities, enterprises, individual learners (yet).

---

## Revenue Model

### B2B SaaS

Bootcamps pay per student per cohort per year.

### Pricing Tiers

| Tier | Price | Duration | Students | Includes |
|------|-------|----------|----------|----------|
| Pilot | ₹2.5-5L ($3-6k) | 4-8 weeks | 30-50 | Full platform, weekly support |
| Growth | ₹99-149/student/year | Annual | 50-200 | Full platform, priority support |
| Enterprise | ₹250-500/student + 3% bonus | Annual | 200+ | Custom features, dedicated support |

### Freemium

Free student tier generates bottom-up demand.

Students pull bootcamps in.

## Pilot Structure

**The pilot IS the product research.**

### Week 1: Onboarding
- Bootcamp sets up cohort
- Students create accounts
- Goal: 80%+ students active

### Week 2: Core Usage
- Students using Orbit
- Roadmaps generated
- Goal: Students asking questions

### Week 3: Mentor Adoption
- Mentors checking dashboard
- Understanding Scores visible
- Goal: Mentors using daily

### Week 4: Measurement
- Scores correlating with outcomes
- Risk alerts firing
- Goal: Data proves value

### Success Criteria
- Mentor adoption > 50%
- Student engagement > 70%
- Score correlates with interview outcomes

### Red Flags
- Mentor adoption < 50%
- Score not correlating
- Orbit unhelpful
- Students not engaging

## Expansion Path

### Stage 1: PSIT Alumni Network (Month 1-3)
- Find alumni at bootcamps
- Get warm intros to founders
- Close 1-3 pilots

### Stage 2: Proof of Concept (Month 3-6)
- Document pilot results
- Create case studies
- Approach bootcamps 2-5 with proof

### Stage 3: Scale (Month 6-12)
- Sales team
- Marketing content
- Partner channel

### Stage 4: Expansion (Year 2+)
- Universities
- Corporate training
- International markets
