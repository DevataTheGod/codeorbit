## CodeOrbit's Unique Position

### What We Do That No One Else Does

1. **Understanding Score** — Quantified comprehension
2. **Socratic AI** — Forces thinking, not copying
3. **Telemetry** — Detects AI-assisted learning
4. **Mentor Intelligence** — Scales mentorship

### What We Don't Do

1. **Generate code** — We refuse to write solutions
2. **Replace mentors** — We scale them
3. **Teach coding** — Bootcamps do that
4. **Replace IDEs** — VS Code is free

## Risks

### Risk 1: Competitor Copies Features

**Mitigation**: Telemetry data is proprietary. Socratic method is hard to replicate well.

### Risk 2: AI Disrupts Verification

**Mitigation**: We use AI to verify, not to generate. AI is a tool, not a replacement.

### Risk 3: Bootcamps Build In-House

**Mitigation**: Building is expensive. Our specialization is verification.

## Positioning Statement

**For bootcamps** who cannot verify student understanding, **CodeOrbit** is a **learning infrastructure platform** that **measures engineering judgment at scale**. Unlike **Replit/Scrimba/Codecademy**, we **verify comprehension, not completion**.
