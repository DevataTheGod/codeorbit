## Why Now

The AI era broke learning verification.

ChatGPT, Copilot, and Cursor enable fake project completion. Bootcamps cannot verify whether students actually understand their work. Mentors cannot scale beyond 20 students.

This problem didn't exist 3 years ago. It's getting worse every month.

## The Problem

**Understanding is unverifiable.**

```
Student submits project
↓
Mentor reviews code (30 mins–2 hours)
↓
Mentor makes assumptions about understanding
↓
Mentor gives feedback
↓
Student passes/fails in interview (4-12 weeks later)
↓
Bootcamp realizes too late if understanding was real
```

The gap between submission and interview outcome is 4–12 weeks. By then, the student is lost.

---

## The Solution

**CodeOrbit measures engineering judgment.**

1. **Understanding Score** — Quantified comprehension, not completion
2. **Orbit AI** — Socratic method forces thinking
3. **Telemetry** — Detects AI-assisted learning
4. **Mentor Intelligence** — Scales mentorship to 100+ students

---

## The Moat

**Pedagogy + Telemetry + Mentor Intelligence**

1. **Pedagogy**: Socratic method is defensible
2. **Telemetry**: Behavioral signals are proprietary
3. **Mentor Intelligence**: Network effects (more students = better models)
4. **Understanding Score**: Industry standard (goal)

Not the IDE. Not the features. The verification system.
